Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2, 2026

My Cash Journal ("we", "our", or "the App") is a financial recording application. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our App.

Key Privacy Principle: This App is designed as a local-first utility. We do not require registration, and your financial transactions are stored locally on your device.

1. Information Collection and Use

a. Financial and Transaction Data

All accounts, categories, and transaction data you input into the App are stored strictly on your local device storage. We do not collect, view, or store your financial data on any external servers.

b. Camera and Media Access

The App requests access to your device's camera and media library solely to allow you to set or change your profile picture. Images selected or taken for your profile picture remain on your local device and are never uploaded to our servers.

c. Device Security and Biometrics

The App supports device-based security features (such as PIN or Biometric authentication) to secure your data. This feature utilizes your device's native secure system. We do not collect, access, or store your biometric data or PIN code.

2. Data Export and Sharing

The App allows you to export your transaction history to your device's Documents folder and share these data files. Any data sharing actions are initiated completely by you (the user), and the data is transmitted directly through your chosen sharing medium.

3. Third-Party Services and Advertisements

To provide certain features, the App uses the following third-party services which may collect information used to identify you in accordance with their own privacy policies:

  • Google Play Services: Used for core app functionalities and checking for the latest app versions.
  • AdMob (Google): The App displays banner advertisements. AdMob may collect and use device identifiers or cookies to serve personalized or non-personalized ads.

4. Internet Permissions

The App requires internet access solely for the following purposes:

  • To check for the latest version of the App.
  • To load and display banner advertisements via AdMob.
  • To facilitate the user-initiated sharing of exported transaction files.

5. Children's Privacy

This App does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided personal information to a third-party service within our app, please contact the respective service provider or us.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
