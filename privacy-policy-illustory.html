IlluStory Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 5, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to IlluStory. This Privacy Policy explains our approach to data and privacy within the IlluStory mobile application.

No Data Collection

IlluStory does not collect, store, transmit, or process any personal information or user data. The app:

  • Does not require user accounts
  • Does not connect to any external servers
  • Does not track user activity
  • Does not use analytics tools

Device Permissions

IlluStory requests the following device permissions solely for app functionality:

  • Storage Access: Required only to access images on your device for use in creating stories
  • Media Library Access: Required only to import images from your device gallery

All images and stories you create remain stored exclusively on your device. We have no ability to access, view, or collect any content you create or import.

Children's Privacy

Since IlluStory does not collect any personal information, it is safe for users of all ages, including children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be included with app updates.

Summary

IlluStory respects your privacy by design. We have intentionally created an app that functions completely offline and does not collect any user information whatsoever.
